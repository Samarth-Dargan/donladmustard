About Me

Donald Mustard is the founder of Chair Entertainment Group, LLC, an American video game company based in Salt Lake City, Utah, as well as being the Chief Creative Officer of Epic Games and Loremaster of Fortnite. Donald Mustard is a spearheading figure in the community of Fortnite, often heading operations and presentations on the game, showcasing it off to fans through his twitter account. He is also well-known for teasing upcoming events and content, including but not limited to: Predator, Superman, and Midas being alive.

He has made cameos so far in Avengers: Endgame and The Matrix Ressurections, both franchises present in Fortnite. Donald Mustard appears in the background of the Battle of Earth in Avengers: Endgame as a sorcerer of the Kamar-Taj, while Tony Stark asks Doctor Strange if this is the timeline where they'll defeat Thanos. Donald Mustard is close friends with The Russo Brothers, directors of Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame. Donald Mustard appears as himself in The Matrix Ressurections, discussing with two other employees during an evacuation protocol about them being "Swatted" by a troll over the latest update. Donald Mustard is a fan of The Matrix series.
